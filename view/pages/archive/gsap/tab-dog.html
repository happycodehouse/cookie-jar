LOVE DOG LIFE

"SAVE THE DOG PROJECT" is a project that
donates a portion of its profits
to conservation efforts

The SAVE THE DOG PROJECT is a project launched by THE DOG COMPANY to commemorate the 20th anniversary of the birth of THE DOG, with the aim of solving various problems related to dogs and cats.
A portion of the sales related to the THE DOG brand business is donated to DOG EAR FUNDS, an NPO animal welfare organization.

What can we do to save the lives of unfortunate dogs and cats? Many people may have the desire to do so, but are unable to take action such as donating.
We have introduced a system that allows those who have never had the opportunity to "donate" before to participate in donations easily and without burden through shopping.

By continuing to accumulate these small efforts, we believe that there will be a future in 30, 50, or 100 years from now where people and beloved dogs and cats live together happily, and we aim for THE DOG to exist to support that lifestyle.
